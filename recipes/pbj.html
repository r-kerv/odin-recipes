Peanut Butter & Jelly sandwich

Home

Description

Think about those days right after school; Just getting home and you're looking for a quick and easy snack. Look no further than this quick and simple PB&J recipe. Check your pantry and let's get started!

Ingredients

  • Peanut butter
  • Jelly (we'll be using grape but use whatever you want)
  • Bread

Steps

  1. Take out your bread of choice
  2. (Optional step) Toast your bread
  3. Take your peanut button and smear it on one slice of bread
  4. On the other slice of bread smear some jelly
  5. This is the most important set ALWAYS CUT THE SANDWICH INTO TRIANGLES
  6. Lastly enjoy!
